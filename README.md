## 🔴 Critical Booking Deadlines

| What | Deadline | Why |
|------|----------|-----|
| **Bariloche NYE hotel** | Book **now** — 12+ months ahead | Dec 31 sells out a full year in advance |
| **All 8 domestic flights** | Book **ASAP** | Dec–Jan is peak season; prices spike |
| **Perito Moreno ice trek** | 3 months before | Limited daily slots on the glacier |
| **Martillo Island penguin tour** | 6–8 weeks before | Max ~20 people per excursion |
| **Mendoza winery tours** | 4 weeks before | January is high season |
| **Península Valdés whale watching** | 2–4 weeks before | Popular December boat slots fill up |
