# Argentina Local Tips

## When to Use
- Preparing culturally before or during travel in Argentina
- Asking about safety, scams, or safe neighbourhoods
- Finding authentic local food experiences beyond tourist traps
- Learning key Spanish phrases and Argentine slang (vos / lunfardo)
- Understanding tipping, payment, and currency customs

## Procedure

### Step 1: Identify the Topic Area
Determine which domain the request covers and load the relevant reference:
- **Culture, customs, etiquette, safety**: [culture-safety.md](./references/culture-safety.md)
- **Food, drinks, language, Argentine Spanish**: [food-language.md](./references/food-language.md)

### Step 2: Apply Regional Context
Tips vary significantly by region:
- **Buenos Aires**: Urban European-influenced city culture, cosmopolitan, fashion-conscious, late-night dining
- **Salta / Northwest**: Strong indigenous and Andean cultural influence, peñas folk music, more traditional pace
- **Mendoza**: Relaxed wine culture, outdoor-focused, Italian-immigrant food influence
- **Patagonia**: Low population, hardy outdoor culture, very different pace from BA; Welsh influence in Chubut (Trelew, Gaiman)
- **Iguazu / Northeast**: Tropical, Guaraní cultural influence, Brazil-Argentina border mixing

### Step 3: Deliver Tips Structured by Category
Organise output as:
1. **Cultural customs & etiquette** (greetings, dinner culture, social norms)
2. **Safety** (city-by-city assessment, practical precautions)
3. **Food & drink** (local specialities, authentic venues, what to order)
4. **Language** (key phrases, Argentine-specific usage)
5. **Money & payments** (tipping, cash culture, exchange)

### Step 4: Flag Common Mistakes and Tourist Traps
Proactively mention:
- Restaurant areas that are tourist-price traps vs. local-price gems
- Common scams (mustard scam, fake police, shortchange)
- Cultural faux pas (refusing mate, being too formal, eating before 9pm)

## Adapting for Other Destinations
The structure (culture, safety, food, language, money) applies universally. Substitute Argentine specifics with destination equivalents.
