# Sample Argentina Itineraries

Use these as starting templates — adjust pace, interests, and available days.

---

## Template 1: Classic Grand Tour (14 days)
*Best for: first-time visitors who want breadth*

**Day 1–3 — Buenos Aires**
- Day 1 arrival: check-in Palermo, evening walk Palermo Soho, parrilla dinner
- Day 2: San Telmo market (morning) → La Boca/Caminito → Puerto Madero waterfront
- Day 3: Recoleta Cemetery → MALBA → tango show evening (book ahead)

**Day 4–5 — Puerto Iguazu**
- Fly Buenos Aires → Iguazu (1.5 hrs)
- Day 4 afternoon: arrive, settle, evening in town
- Day 5: Full day Argentine side — Upper & Lower Circuits, Devil's Throat, Macuco boat tour

**Day 6–9 — Salta & Northwest**
- Fly Iguazu → Salta (1.5 hrs direct)
- Day 6: Salta city — MAAM museum, Plaza 9 de Julio, peñas (folk music evening)
- Day 7: Quebrada de Humahuaca (Tilcara, Purmamarca 7 Colours Hill, Humahuaca) — full day tour or hire car
- Day 8: Salinas Grandes salt flats + afternoon rest (altitude ~3,450m)
- Day 9: Cafayate wine valley — torrontés tasting, Quebrada de las Conchas (coloured rocks)

**Day 10–12 — Mendoza**
- Fly Salta → Mendoza (2 hrs, often via Buenos Aires)
- Day 10 afternoon: arrive, city walk, plaza evening
- Day 11: Luján de Cuyo / Maipú wine tasting (hire driver or bike tour)
- Day 12: Aconcagua park Horcones hike OR rafting at Potrerillos + Uco Valley winery lunch

**Day 13–14 — Buenos Aires (return)**
- Fly Mendoza → Buenos Aires
- Day 13: Palermo parks, Recoleta neighbourhood, final shopping
- Day 14: Morning free, depart from EZE or AEP

**Flights**: BA–IGA, IGA–SLA, SLA–MDZ (or via EZE), MDZ–EZE. Book domestic legs early — Aerolíneas and Flybondi.

## Template 2: Patagonia Focus (10 days)
*Best for: nature lovers, trekkers, glacier chasers*

**Day 1 — Buenos Aires (transit)**
- Arrive EZE, transfer to Palermo hotel
- Evening steak dinner, early night

**Day 2–4 — Bariloche**
- Fly Buenos Aires → Bariloche (2 hrs)
- Day 2: Circuito Chico scenic drive, Cerro Campanario chairlift at sunset
- Day 3: Nahuel Huapi full-day trek (Refugio Jakob or Cerro Catedral trails)
- Day 4: Llao Llao peninsula walk, chocolate shops, craft brewery evening

**Day 5–7 — El Calafate**
- Fly Bariloche → El Calafate (1.5 hrs)
- Day 5 afternoon: arrive, Nimez Lagoon flamingo walk at dusk
- Day 6: Perito Moreno Glacier — full day: walkways (2 hrs) + boat nautical safari (1 hr) + Mini Trekking (ice walk — book in advance)
- Day 7: Lago Argentino full-day boat cruise (Upsala & Spegazzini glaciers) OR rest day + town

**Day 8–9 — El Chaltén**
- Bus El Calafate → El Chaltén (3 hrs)
- Day 8: Laguna Torre trek (full day, moderate)
- Day 9: Laguna de los Tres / Fitz Roy trek (full day, strenuous) — weather-dependent

**Day 10 — Ushuaia or Buenos Aires**
- Option A: Bus back El Calafate, fly Ushuaia for 1 night (add 2 more days for proper Ushuaia experience)
- Option B: Bus back El Calafate, fly Buenos Aires same day for departure

**Note**: Weather in El Chaltén is extremely variable. Book accommodation flexibly. Cash from El Calafate mandatory (no ATM in El Chaltén).

## Template 3: Buenos Aires + Wine Country (6–7 days)
*Best for: urban explorers and food/wine enthusiasts*

**Day 1–4 — Buenos Aires (deep dive)**
- Day 1: Arrival, Palermo neighbourhood, parrilla dinner
- Day 2: San Telmo → La Boca (morning) → MALBA (afternoon) → Puerto Madero (evening)
- Day 3: Recoleta Cemetery → café Tortoni → evening tango milonga (La Catedral or El Beso — authentic, not touristy)
- Day 4: Tigre Delta day trip OR Palermo parks + football stadium tour

**Day 5–7 — Mendoza**
- Fly Buenos Aires → Mendoza (1.5 hrs)
- Day 5: Arrive, city centre walk, regional dinner (chivo asado — roast goat)
- Day 6: Full-day wine circuit: Maipú (bike tour) or Luján (driver) — 4–5 wineries
- Day 7: Uco Valley (Zuccardi, Clos de los Siete) — high-altitude premium bodegas; fly back evening

**Best winery picks by category**:
- Mass market / accessible: Norton, Trapiche (Maipú)
- Premium: Catena Zapata, Cheval des Andes, Achaval Ferrer (Luján)
- Boutique / views: Zuccardi Valle de Uco, Clos de los Siete (Uco Valley)

## Template 4: Northern Adventure — Salta + Iguazu (9 days)
*Best for: culture, history, UNESCO sites, natural wonders*

**Day 1–2 — Buenos Aires (gateway)**
- Day 1: Arrive, Palermo/Recoleta exploration, steak dinner
- Day 2: San Telmo market, city highlights, fly afternoon to Salta (1.5 hrs)

**Day 3–6 — Salta & Northwest**
- Day 3: Salta city — MAAM (mummies), colonial architecture, peña evening
- Day 4: Quebrada de Humahuaca (Tilcara, Purmamarca, Uquía church)
- Day 5: Salinas Grandes + afternoon Jujuy city
- Day 6: Cafayate (4 hrs south of Salta) — wine tasting + Quebrada de las Conchas — return evening

**Day 7–9 — Iguazu Falls**
- Fly Salta → Iguazu (1.5–2 hrs, often via Buenos Aires)
- Day 7: Arrive afternoon, evening in Puerto Iguazu town
- Day 8: Full day Argentine side — all circuits + Macuco boat
- Day 9: Optional Brazilian side (half day) then fly Buenos Aires for connection home

## Template 5: Short Break — Buenos Aires (3–4 days)
*Best for: long weekenders, stopovers, first-time Argentina visitors*

**Day 1 — Arrival + Palermo**
- Arrive, check in Palermo
- Afternoon: Palermo park walk, Bosques de Palermo
- Evening: Parrilla dinner in Palermo Soho (Don Julio or La Carnicería — book ahead)

**Day 2 — History & Culture**
- Morning: Recoleta Cemetery (free, 2 hrs), Recoleta cultural centre
- Afternoon: MALBA (1.5 hrs) or Bellas Artes (free)
- Evening: San Telmo tapas bar crawl → milonga (tango watching or lesson)

**Day 3 — Waterfront & Boca**
- Morning: La Boca / Caminito (go before 11am — less crowded)
- Afternoon: Puerto Madero waterfront, Ecological Reserve walk, Faena arts district
- Evening: Puerto Madero parrilla or upscale restaurant

**Day 4 (if available)**
- Half day: Tigre Delta (train from Retiro, 1 hr → boat tour)
- Afternoon: Return, last shopping in Palermo (leather goods, wine, dulce de leche)
- Evening: flight or hotel departure

**Buenos Aires pro tips**:
- Taxis: Use official remises or InDriver/Cabify app (avoid street taxis at night)
- Transport: SUBE card for bus + metro (buy at any kiosk)
- Dinner time: Locals eat at 9pm–midnight — restaurants half-empty at 7pm
- Currency: Use official exchange (casas de cambio or bank transfer at "blue" rate — check current regulations)
